Our Mission, Values & Approach

Our Mission

01

I started SoundIn because I wanted to build things I believe in, with people I respect. Web, events, software, lighting — different disciplines, one standard of care. Every client I take on gets me and my team genuinely invested in what they’re trying to do.

Highland Roots

02

I started SoundIn in the Scottish Highlands because that’s where I live — not as a brand story, but as a fact that shapes how I see time, scope, and what good work actually means. Emma and Mark bring everything I can’t do alone.

You don’t build slowly here by accident. The distance from the noise is a feature. It keeps me honest about what I take on and who I take it on for.

Our Approach

03

Six practices isn’t a portfolio strategy. It’s what happens when you come from production, know how to build software, and refuse to hand off the parts you find inconvenient.

When a client needs lighting and a website and someone to run their socials, I don’t outsource it. I do the work — or I bring in Emma and Mark, who are as invested as I am.

Values

04

Say what you mean. Charge what the work costs. Own your mistakes. Take on less so you can do more with it. Build things that outlast the brief. Care about the people on the other side of every contract — and about the communities they operate in.

That’s not a values framework. That’s just how I prefer to work.
