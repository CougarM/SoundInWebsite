Our Mission

01

We build things we believe in, with people we respect. Web, events, software, lighting — different disciplines, one standard of care. Every client we take on gets a founding team that is genuinely invested in what they're trying to do.

Highland Roots

02

Cougar, Emma, and Mark started SoundIn in the Scottish Highlands because that's where we live — not as a brand story, but as a fact that shapes how we see time, scope, and what good work actually means.

You don't build slowly here by accident. The distance from the noise is a feature. It keeps us honest about what we take on and who we take it on for.

Our Approach

03

Six practices isn't a portfolio strategy. It's the natural result of three people who each came from different disciplines and chose not to abandon any of them.

That breadth means when a client needs lighting and a website and someone to run their socials, we don't outsource the parts we find inconvenient. We do the work.

Values

04

Say what you mean. Charge what the work costs. Own your mistakes. Take on less so you can do more with it. Build things that outlast the brief. Care about the people on the other side of every contract — and about the communities they operate in.

That's not a values framework. That's just how we prefer to work.
